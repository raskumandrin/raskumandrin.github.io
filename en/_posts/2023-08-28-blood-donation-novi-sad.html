Address of the Blood Transfusion Station in Novi Sad: Hajduk Veljkova 9a, Novi Sad 21000

Website: http://www.transfuzija.rs/

Requirements for foreign citizens:

  • Reside in Serbia for six months
  • Have a JMBG - ID from the residence permit
  • Passport

First, you need to fill out a standard form. They check your hemoglobin and blood pressure. Upon completion, they provide water, a roll, and a snack. Blood donation is allowed no more than once every three months. You also receive a certificate for your employer to grant you two days off. No payment is provided for donating blood.
